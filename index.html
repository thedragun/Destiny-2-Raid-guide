A Destiny 2 Raid Guide

A standard guide to destiny 2's seven raids

Leviathan


Capture the flag

Its time to play a game of capture the flag.

The starting point changes every reset each door leads to an activity. you need to split your team into two groups.

One group is going to capture flags from standard bearers around the map and deliver the flags to the door of the activity that your going to.

Meanwhile the other team will be defending the captured flags from cabal trying to retake the flag.
